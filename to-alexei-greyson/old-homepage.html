About us

Hello, this is a forum for endeavors who are investigating the identity of "Alexei Greyson". During the 1980s, 10 years after the War ended, a certain number of government documents were requested to be publicized by Mr. Edward Bridge as one of the promises to the people for his campaign. He was successfully elected General Administrator and many soon read through the hundreds of files, especially theorists and government protesters. The heat soon died as a lot came to the realization that the documents were rather boring and little to no "secret" government information was found, either rendering Mr. Bridge sly or the gov truthful to its citizens.
Either way, our community is more piqued by the frequent mention of a person, with a name or possibly pseudonym, 'Alexei Greyson'. This man was frequently involved in multiple missions, treaties, and all sorts of projects that contributed to the ongoing peace treaties between the nation.
Please note that we are aware he is still alive and we do not wish to interfere with his personal life.
